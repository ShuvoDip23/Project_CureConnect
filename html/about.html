About CureConnect

Idea Behind CureConnect

Finding the right doctor can be a challenging task, especially when people are unsure about which specialist to consult for their health concerns. CureConnect was created to bridge this gap by using natural language processing (NLP) to analyze symptoms and suggest possible health conditions. By manually curating a list of trusted doctors, we aim to make healthcare more accessible and less overwhelming for users. Our goal is to empower individuals with the right information, helping them take the first step toward proper medical care.

Issues in Bangladesh to Find Doctors

Many people in Bangladesh struggle to find the right doctor due to a lack of centralized information. Patients often rely on word-of-mouth recommendations, which may not always lead to the best medical care. Additionally, misinformation, long waiting times, and the challenge of knowing which specialist to visit can cause delays in treatment. Rural areas, in particular, suffer from a lack of specialist doctors, making healthcare even more difficult to access.

How CureConnect is Solving These Issues

CureConnect simplifies the process of finding the right doctor by using NLP-based symptom analysis to suggest possible health conditions and relevant specialists. At this stage, we are manually adding trusted doctors to ensure accuracy and reliability. By doing so, we help reduce confusion, save time, and improve access to quality healthcare for users across Bangladesh.

Our Vision for the Future

Currently, we are manually curating doctor information, but our long-term vision is to develop a structured database and integrate AI for more precise health recommendations. Our goal is to revolutionize healthcare accessibility in Bangladesh by making reliable medical information easily available to everyone.
